Vis-à-vis

2021

A platform for debating topics in a orderly manner

Web design

View the website

Description

This is a group project from school. the task we were going to solve were; how do we make more 18-35 year olds read 'aftenposten'. 'Aftenposten' is Norway's largest printed newspaper by circulation, and was founded in 1860. their readers mostly consist of elderly readers nowadays. They want to expand their reach into younger audiences.Our solution were to make an app where younger people can debate in a orderly manner, and they can learn proper debating techniques and behaviour. That lead our group to the concept 'vis-à-vis'. vis-à-vis is a french term and it means to face something or to face each other. the norwegian word for newspaper is 'avis'.
