Description

I was tasked with developing a useful app, which will help you who travel both domestically and abroad.  The app will help you to discover what the place you are going to visit, has to offer. The end result was based on information gathered from qualitative and quantitative interviews.  The app works by filling in different criterias;- What you want to do: eat, drink, shop. - when you want to do it: from/to a specific time.- what you want to spend: from/to a specific amount of money.- what distance you want to walk: A specific distance from you to what you wanna do.
